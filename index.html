About me

I’m a UX Designer skilled in Product Design idea to conception with over 6 years of experience, and the last 2 years focused on technology products, User Interface and Experience.

I started out as an industrial designer and worked across multiple industry sectors with companies in the UK, USA, Europe and SEA, consulting and creating designs. I have worked for 3 startups overseeing the whole product development and many other duties as an early stage startup founder including leadership and fundraising.

I’ve been spreading my enthusiasm for creating products and experiences by hosting a Design Mastermind where I share my knowledge and provide a space for collaboration.

I’m passionate about applying consumer psychology to an interaction science-backed approach. My background in engineering allows me to plan for worst-case scenarios and focus on details when solving problems.
